# StadiumIQ
Your smart venue companion. Know before you go. Move before the crowd.

## Challenge Vertical
Physical Event Experience — Large-Scale Sporting Venues

## Problem Statement
Large sporting venues with 50,000+ attendees face three 
critical challenges: unmanaged crowd movement causing 
dangerous density build-ups, long unpredictable wait times 
at food and restroom facilities, and lack of real-time 
coordination between attendees and venue operations.

## How StadiumIQ Addresses the Challenge
- **Crowd Movement**: Live zone density heatmap shows 
  attendees which areas are congested in real time
- **Waiting Times**: Queue wait time estimates for all 
  food courts and restrooms, sorted shortest-first
- **Real-time Coordination**: AI assistant proactively 
  alerts attendees at half-time and final 10 minutes 
  with personalized routing advice
- **Seamless Experience**: Single deployable URL, 
  no app install, works on any device
